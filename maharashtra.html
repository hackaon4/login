About Maharashtra

Maharashtra is India's second-most populous state and third-largest state by area. Spread over 307,713 km², it's bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west and the Indian states of Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana, Karnataka, and Goa.

The state has a rich history dating back to the 3rd century BCE, with the region being ruled by various dynasties including the Mauryas, Satavahanas, Rashtrakutas, Chalukyas, and Marathas.

Today, Maharashtra is India's most industrialized state and a leader in sectors like finance, entertainment (Bollywood), technology, and manufacturing.
